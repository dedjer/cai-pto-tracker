PTO Tracker

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1) Go to PeopleSoft Time Reporting
Step 2) Log in with your Cox Automotive Employee ID and Password
Step 3) Expand "Leave and Compensatory Time Balances" Section
Step 4) Click this PTO Chrome Extension in your browser to see remaining PTO days
Step 5) Repeat this process any time you take PTO or each month because your remaining hours will change

NOTE: If you'd like to have this extention figure out your PTO based on years of service, go to the "Options" page by right-clicking this extention in the toolbar and selecting "Options". You can set your start date and an option to display your remaining PTO days as a notification badge.
